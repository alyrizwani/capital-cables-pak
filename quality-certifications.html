International Standards

Our Certifications

Every certification represents hundreds of tests, rigorous audits, and an unwavering commitment to manufacturing excellence.

ISO 9001

Quality Management System certification ensuring every process from raw material sourcing to finished product delivery meets international standards.

IEC

International Electrotechnical Commission standards governing electrical and electronic technologies, ensuring global compliance and safety.

BSS

British Standards Specification compliance for cable manufacturing, one of the most rigorous and respected electrical safety frameworks worldwide.

UL Certified

Underwriters Laboratories certification for advanced flame retardancy, thermal tolerance, and comprehensive electrical safety across all product lines.

PCSIR

Pakistan Council of Scientific and Industrial Research laboratory testing — the nation's most trusted independent verification body for industrial products.

PSQCA

Pakistan Standards and Quality Control Authority certification verifying compliance with all national standards for cable and wire manufacturing.

Our quality control process begins at raw material intake and continues through every stage of production. Every batch is subject to independent third-party verification before dispatch.

  • High-voltage insulation resistance testing

  • Mechanical strength and elongation testing
  • Temperature rating and thermal aging tests
  • Full batch traceability documentation
